Introduction

Following the recommendations by the United Nations Statistical Commission (decision 42/102) in 2013, the minimum set of gender indicators composed of 52 quantitative indicators and 11 qualitative indicators covering national norms and laws on gender equality. The indicators are to be used a guide for the national production and international compilation of gender statistics.

The indicators are classified into the three tiers:


Tier 1

Conceptually clear, with an agreed international definition and regularly produced by countries.

Tier 2

Conceptually clear, with an agreed international definition, but not yet regularly produced by countries.

Tier 3

No agreed international standards, not regularly produced by countries.
